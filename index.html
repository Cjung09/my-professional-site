Overview

Algorand is the world's most decentralized, scalable, and secure blockchain infrastructure. It has everything from generative NFT art, decentralized finance, and more. Furthermore, Algorand is committed to sustainability and has become the world's first carbon-negative blockchain.

Decentralized Exchange

Users can trade on Decentralized Exchanges (DEX) such as Tinyman by connecting their Algorand Wallet to the platform. There are many options to stake your tokens to earn a specified yield as a reward for doing so.

NFT Collections

Users can buy and sell NFTs made by artists on NFT marketplaces such as Randgallery or NFTxAlgo.
